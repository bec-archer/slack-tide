Project Overview & Implementation Costs
qrstkr.com • Hydraulics SR-80
Project Goal

Tag any equipment with a scannable QR code sticker tied to a digital record that tracks ownership, maintenance, photos, and specs. When the item changes hands, the record goes with it. Revenue comes from sticker sales, annual subscriptions, and eventually a marketplace.

Who Uses It
👤

Owners

Regular people who buy stickers for their stuff. They log maintenance, upload photos, and manage their items. Our primary customer.

🔧

Repair Shops

Mechanics and service providers scan the sticker and log work performed. Adds third-party credibility to the maintenance history.

🏪

Dealers

Car, boat, and equipment dealers order stickers in bulk and put them on every unit. Buyers get them at point of sale.

🔍

Buyers

Anyone considering a used item scans the sticker to see its full public history, photos, and specs before buying.
